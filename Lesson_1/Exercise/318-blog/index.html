Department 318 , Chemistry, Manufacturing and Control (CMC) Bioanalysis, is located in CMC Drug Product Development, R&D, Novo Nordisk A/S. We use and optimize bioanalysis to support a range of developing medicines in diabetes, haemophilia and obesity. The department works with cell-based bioassays, clotting and enzymatic assays as well as ELISA assays and different molecular biology methods. There are three teams in the department,...
